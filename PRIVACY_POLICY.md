# Privacy Policy — Web Notes Assistant

**Last Updated:** April 2026  
**Extension:** Web Notes Assistant  
**Developer:** YuvaTech  

---

## Overview

Web Notes Assistant is a Firefox browser extension that lets you capture, organize, and export notes from web pages. We are committed to your privacy. This policy explains what data the extension accesses, how it is stored, and what is never collected.

---

## Data We Access

### 1. Text You Highlight
When you select text on a webpage and click "Add to Notes", the selected text and its source URL are saved.

### 2. Screenshots You Capture
When you use the screenshot tool to select a region of a page, that image is captured and saved as a note.

### 3. Page URLs and Titles
The URL and title of the page you are viewing are stored alongside each note for reference.

---

## How Data Is Stored

- **All data is stored locally on your device** using Firefox's `browser.storage.local` API.
- **Nothing is ever sent to any external server**, cloud service, or third party.
- The developer has **no access whatsoever** to your notes, workspaces, or browsing activity.

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal information.
- We do not use analytics or tracking.
- We do not run ads or share data with advertisers.
- We do not transmit any data outside your browser.

---

## Permissions Used

| Permission | Reason |
|------------|--------|
| `activeTab` | To inject the sidebar panel into the current tab |
| `storage` | To save your notes and workspace settings locally |
| `tabs` | To retrieve the current page URL and title for note metadata |
| `<all_urls>` | To allow the extension to work on any website you visit |

---

## Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time by:
1. Opening the extension sidebar
2. Deleting each workspace manually, or
3. Going to `about:addons` → Web Notes Assistant → Clear Data

Uninstalling the extension will also remove all locally stored data.
